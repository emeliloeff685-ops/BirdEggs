## Description
(4000 characters max - NO EMOJI, NO MARKDOWN)

Bird Eggs - Catch the Eggs, Avoid the Bombs!

Get ready for an exciting arcade adventure where you control a bird trying to catch as many eggs as possible! Bird Eggs is a fast-paced, action-packed game that will test your reflexes and hand-eye coordination. Perfect for quick gaming sessions or extended play!

GAMEPLAY

Control your bird by tapping and dragging across the screen. Catch falling eggs to earn points, but watch out for bombs! Each bomb you hit will cost you a life. Survive until the timer runs out to win the round. The game gets more challenging as you progress, keeping you engaged and entertained.

KEY FEATURES

Three Difficulty Levels - Choose from Easy, Medium, or Hard to match your skill level. Easy mode is perfect for beginners with more time and lives. Medium mode offers a balanced challenge for experienced players. Hard mode provides the ultimate test of skill and reflexes.

Lives System - Start with multiple lives and try to keep as many as possible for the best score. Each bomb collision costs you a life, so stay alert and focused!

Score Tracking - Compete with yourself and beat your personal best. Track your progress and see your game history with detailed statistics.

Statistics - View your best scores, total games played, win and loss records, and complete game history. Monitor your improvement over time.

Beautiful Graphics - Enjoy colorful, vibrant visuals and smooth animations that make every game session visually appealing.

Relaxing Music - Immerse yourself in the game with background music that enhances your gaming experience.

Customizable Settings - Turn music, sound effects, and vibration on or off according to your preferences.

HOW TO PLAY

1. Select your preferred difficulty level - Easy, Medium, or Hard
2. Tap and drag to move your bird left or right across the screen
3. Catch eggs to score points - each egg is worth 10 points
4. Avoid bombs or you'll lose a life - stay focused and react quickly
5. Survive until time runs out to win the round!

CHALLENGE YOURSELF

Easy Mode - Perfect for beginners with more time and lives. Great for learning the game mechanics and building confidence.

Medium Mode - A balanced challenge for experienced players. Test your skills with moderate difficulty and timing.

Hard Mode - For the ultimate test of skill and reflexes. Only the most skilled players can master this challenging mode.

TRACK YOUR PROGRESS

View your statistics to see your best scores, total games played, win and loss records, and complete game history. Monitor your improvement and set new personal records.

Simple and intuitive controls make the game easy to learn but challenging to master. Multiple difficulty levels ensure there's always a challenge that matches your skill level. Score tracking and statistics help you monitor your progress. Beautiful, colorful graphics provide an enjoyable visual experience. Smooth gameplay ensures responsive controls and fluid animations. No ads or in-app purchases - enjoy the full game experience without interruptions. Offline play means no internet connection is required - play anywhere, anytime.
