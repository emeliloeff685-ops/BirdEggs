## Description
(4000 characters max - NO EMOJI, NO MARKDOWN)

Bird Eggs - Catch the Eggs, Avoid the Bombs!

Get ready for an exciting arcade adventure where you control a bird trying to catch as many eggs as possible! Bird Eggs is a fast-paced, action-packed game that will test your reflexes and hand-eye coordination.

GAMEPLAY

Control your bird by tapping and dragging across the screen. Catch falling eggs to earn points, but watch out for bombs! Each bomb you hit will cost you a life. Survive until the timer runs out to win the round.

KEY FEATURES

Three Difficulty Levels - Choose from Easy, Medium, or Hard to match your skill level. Easy mode is perfect for beginners. Medium mode offers a balanced challenge. Hard mode provides the ultimate test of skill and reflexes.

Lives System - Start with multiple lives and try to keep as many as possible for the best score. Each bomb collision costs you a life.

Score Tracking - Compete with yourself and beat your personal best. Track your progress with detailed statistics.

Statistics - View your best scores, total games played, win and loss records, and complete game history.

Beautiful Graphics - Enjoy colorful, vibrant visuals and smooth animations.

Relaxing Music - Immerse yourself in the game with background music.

Customizable Settings - Turn music, sound effects, and vibration on or off.

HOW TO PLAY

1. Select your preferred difficulty level - Easy, Medium, or Hard
2. Tap and drag to move your bird left or right
3. Catch eggs to score points - each egg is worth 10 points
4. Avoid bombs or you'll lose a life
5. Survive until time runs out to win!

CHALLENGE YOURSELF

Easy Mode - Perfect for beginners with more time and lives.

Medium Mode - A balanced challenge for experienced players.

Hard Mode - For the ultimate test of skill and reflexes.

FEATURES

Simple and intuitive controls. Multiple difficulty levels. Score tracking and statistics. Beautiful, colorful graphics. Smooth gameplay. No ads or in-app purchases. Offline play - no internet required.

Perfect for quick gaming sessions during breaks, while waiting, commuting, or whenever you want to relax and have fun. Bird Eggs offers endless entertainment for players of all ages.

Download now and start catching eggs! Can you beat your high score?
